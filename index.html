The NexGen Story

Travel that feels personal

NexGen Bookings was built on a simple observation: the kosher travel market was being served by agents who treated it as a niche add-on, not a speciality. Clients were getting generic itineraries with a kosher restaurant list stapled to the end.

We do something different. We handle every aspect of your trip — flights, hotels, transfers, dining, and all the logistics that observant travellers actually think about — as one integrated plan, built from scratch around your life.

Every client gets a digital itinerary: a clean, organised brief with every confirmation and detail accessible before and during the trip. One point of contact. Full accountability. No surprises.

Where We Go

The places we
know well.

Every itinerary is custom. These are destinations we've planned enough times to know what makes them work — and what to avoid.

Europe · France

Paris

The Marais. La Victoire. Shabbat within walking distance. September is the month.

Europe · Italy

Rome

The oldest ghetto in Europe. Remarkable history, remarkable food. Book early.

Europe · Italy

Venice

The Ghetto Novo, founded 1516. For special occasions. Unique in the world.

Middle East · UAE

Dubai

Since normalisation, genuinely excellent kosher options. Friday afternoons need planning.

Mediterranean · Cyprus

Paphos

A relaxed coastal escape. Easy from Israel. Good for a long weekend.

Americas · Panama

Panama City

A surprisingly strong Jewish community and kosher infrastructure. Genuinely different.

Caribbean · Mexico

Cancún

Resorts that work with kosher caterers. White sand. Useful for families.

Europe · Italy

Milan

Fashion, design, and an underrated Jewish community. The Duomo and then dinner.

Europe · UK

London

Golders Green. Edgware. A kosher infrastructure that's hard to beat anywhere in Europe.

Europe · Netherlands

Amsterdam

The Portuguese Synagogue. The Anne Frank House. Jewish Amsterdam is its own journey.

Mediterranean · Spain

Barcelona

Architecture, beaches, and a growing kosher scene. Best in May or October.

Europe · Hungary

Budapest

The Dohány Street Synagogue — the largest in Europe. Grand architecture. Affordable.

Europe · Czech Republic

Prague

Josefov — one of the best-preserved Jewish quarters in the world. Stunning at any season.

Middle East · Israel

Jerusalem

The Western Wall, the Old City, and an unmatched spiritual weight. There is nowhere else like it.

Americas · USA

New York

The Five Towns. The Upper West Side. For destination clients flying in from abroad.

Americas · USA

Miami

Bal Harbour. South Beach. Year-round sun with a strong kosher community built in.

Mediterranean · Morocco

Marrakech

The Mellah, the souks, and a surprisingly accessible kosher infrastructure. Genuinely different.

Europe · Portugal

Lisbon

Alfama, the trams, the Atlantic light. A small but growing kosher scene — and one of Europe's most beautiful cities.

Asia · Thailand

Thailand

Chabad houses in Bangkok, Chiang Mai, and Koh Samui. Stunning country with real support for kosher travellers.

Africa · South Africa

Cape Town

Lion's Head, the Cape Peninsula, and a well-established Jewish community. One of the most beautiful cities in the world.

How it works

Step by Step

From your first message
to your boarding pass.

1

You share your travel details

Destination, dates, group size, budget, kosher requirements, and anything specific — special occasions, accessibility needs, neighbourhoods you want to be near. We ask the questions a good travel adviser asks.

2

We build your itinerary

A real person researches and curates your trip — flights, hotels, kosher dining, transfers, and logistics. We build it around your preferences, not around what's easiest for us to book.

3

You review everything

We send a full proposal. You review it. We adjust until every detail is right. Nothing is booked until you say so — and every decision is explained.

4

We handle all the bookings

Once approved, we manage every reservation — flights, hotels, transfers, restaurants, and any special arrangements. You don't need to do anything except pack.

5

Your digital itinerary arrives

A clean digital brief with every confirmation, address, and detail — organised and accessible from your phone before and during the trip. Clients reference it constantly.
