Who we are

NexGen Bookings is a kosher travel concierge and booking service dedicated to creating seamless, affordable, and elevated travel experiences for the Jewish community. We specialize in planning and booking kosher-friendly vacations that combine comfort, value, and attention to detail—without the high price tag typically associated with full-service travel planning.

Our focus is simple: we handle every aspect of your trip, so you don't have to. From flights and hotels to transportation and curated travel experiences, we manage your travel from start to finish while ensuring your destination meets your kosher and Jewish lifestyle needs.

At NexGen Bookings, we believe kosher travel should be accessible, well-planned, and stress-free. Whether you're traveling for a family vacation, honeymoon, or special occasion, we tailor each trip to your preferences while securing great value and reliable accommodations.

As part of our concierge service, every client receives a beautiful, app-based digital itinerary, giving you instant access to your complete travel plan in one organized place. Your reservations, confirmations, and essential trip details are always at your fingertips—before and during your journey.

Our goal is to deliver a luxury-feel vacation at an affordable price, with personalized service, transparent planning, and a deep understanding of kosher travel requirements.

How It Works

Planning your next trip should be simple, stress-free, and tailored to your needs. Here's how working with Nexgen Bookings works from start to finish.

1

Share Your Travel Details

Begin by submitting a request through our Get Started form. Tell us where you want to go, your preferred travel dates, budget range, and any specific preferences — including kosher requirements, accommodations, flights, or special experiences.

2

We Design Your Trip

We carefully research and curate a personalized travel plan based on your needs. This may include flights, hotels or resorts, kosher dining options, tours, and logistics — all selected to match your preferences and travel style.

3

Review & Approve

You'll receive a custom proposal to review. We'll make adjustments as needed until everything feels right. Nothing is booked without your approval.

4

We Handle the Bookings

Once approved, we handle all the bookings and details on your behalf — flights, hotels, transfers, and any special arrangements — so you don't have to lift a finger.

5

Receive Your Digital Itinerary

You'll receive a beautiful, app-based digital itinerary with all your confirmations, reservations, and trip details organized in one place — accessible before and during your journey.
