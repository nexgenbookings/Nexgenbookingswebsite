The Process

How It Works

Simple, honest, and completely tailored to you. Here's exactly how we bring your trip to life.

1

Share your travel details

Submit your request through our form. Tell us your destination, travel dates, group size, budget, and any specific preferences — including kosher requirements, accommodations, or special experiences you have in mind.

2

We design your trip

We research and curate a personalised travel plan — flights, hotels, kosher dining options, transfers, and logistics — all selected to match your preferences and travel style. A real person does this work.

3

Review & refine

You'll receive a custom proposal to review. We adjust until everything feels exactly right. Nothing gets booked without your full approval.

4

We handle everything

Once approved, we handle all the bookings — flights, hotels, transfers, and any special arrangements — so you don't have to lift a finger.

5

Your digital itinerary arrives

A beautiful app-based itinerary with all your confirmations in one organised place — accessible before and throughout your journey.
