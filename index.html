The NexGen Story

Travel That Feels Personal

NexGen Bookings is a kosher travel concierge dedicated to creating seamless, affordable, and elevated travel experiences for the Jewish community. We specialize in planning and booking kosher-friendly vacations that combine comfort, value, and attention to detail — without the high price tag typically associated with full-service travel planning.

Our focus is simple: we handle every aspect of your trip so you don't have to. From flights and hotels to transportation and curated experiences, we manage everything from start to finish while ensuring your destination meets your kosher and Jewish lifestyle needs.

Every client receives a beautiful, app-based digital itinerary — your complete travel plan in one organized, always-accessible place. Reservations, confirmations, and trip details right at your fingertips, before and during your journey.

The Process

How It Works

Simple, stress-free, and completely tailored to you. Here's exactly how we bring your trip to life.

Step by Step

From First Message to Dream Trip

We've refined a simple process that gets you from "I want to travel" to boarding your flight — with zero stress in between.

1

Share Your Travel Details

Submit your request through our Get Started form. Tell us your destination, travel dates, group size, budget, and any specific preferences — including kosher requirements, accommodations, or special experiences.

2

We Design Your Trip

We research and curate a personalized travel plan based on your needs — flights, hotels, kosher dining options, tours, and logistics — all selected to match your preferences and travel style.

3

Review & Refine

You'll receive a custom proposal to review. We'll make any adjustments until everything feels exactly right. Nothing gets booked without your full approval.

4

We Handle Everything

Once approved, we handle all the bookings on your behalf — flights, hotels, transfers, and any special arrangements — so you don't have to lift a finger.

5

Receive Your Digital Itinerary

You'll receive a beautiful, app-based digital itinerary with all your confirmations organized in one place — accessible before and throughout your journey.
